TBA

TBA stands for Token Bound Account — a unique wallet tied to an NFT. At BizenDAO, we give each Bizen-yaki NFT its own TBA, using it like a traditional "Hakogaki" (box inscription). Hakogaki refers to inscriptions on an artwork's storage box, where the artist and successive owners record the title, provenance, and signatures of the piece.
