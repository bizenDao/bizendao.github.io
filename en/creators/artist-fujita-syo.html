Fujita Syo

Expressing the natural beauty of Bizen clay as it truly is cannot be achieved overnight. We refuse to compromise in selecting our clay, taking the time and care to prepare it properly, and pursuing forms and expressions that best suit each unique material. Even if the result overturns conventional ideas of Bizen ware, we remain committed to confronting its true essence. Beyond the traditional concept of “the beauty of utility,” we seek to create creative Bizen ware that transcends boundaries and fixed forms.

About Shiro-Bizen (White Bizen)

About Shiro-Bizen (White Bizen)

I find beauty in the way the grain of clay and subtle shadows emerge within the quietness of white. Unlike glazed surfaces, this is the inherent whiteness of the material itself — I focus on revealing it through form and light. (Provisional)
