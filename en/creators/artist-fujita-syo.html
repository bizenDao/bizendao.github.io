Fujita Sho

Fujita Sho is an artist who perceives the layers of clay texture and kiln-born expression as "landscape," creating vessels that embody the accumulation of time. While honoring the traditions of tea ceremony, he pursues contours and negative space that harmonize with contemporary life. (Provisional)

About Shiro-Bizen (White Bizen)

About Shiro-Bizen (White Bizen)

I find beauty in the way the grain of clay and subtle shadows emerge within the quietness of white. Unlike glazed surfaces, this is the inherent whiteness of the material itself — I focus on revealing it through form and light. (Provisional)
