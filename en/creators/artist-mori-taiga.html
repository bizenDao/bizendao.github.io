Mori Taiga

Mori Taiga is an artist who reinterprets the "chance" born from Bizen clay and flame as contemporary form. Balancing raw, bold surfaces with quiet negative space, he designs texture and shadow with intention, creating vessels that accompany the rhythms of their users' daily lives.

My Definition of Bizen Pottery

I believe the allure of Bizen-yaki lies in the way fire completes the work beyond the maker's intentions. While embracing that element of chance, I carefully refine the form, balance, and tactile quality of the rim — the parts a user touches every day. I believe that striking this balance is the true value of Bizen pottery in the modern era. (Provisional)
