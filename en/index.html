About Bizen-yaki→

Nihon Rokkoyō

The Six Ancient Kilns of Japan

The Six Ancient Kilns is a collective name for six representative ceramic production regions that have been in continuous operation since the medieval period.
In spring 2017, they were designated as a Japan Heritage site.
Bizen-yaki, a traditional craft of Okayama Prefecture, is one of the six.

Our Vision

Bizen-yaki — one of the Six Ancient Kilns designated as a Japan Heritage site, with over a thousand years of history.
We are committed to preserving and advancing this traditional craft culture.

Our Activities

BizenDAO is a community where potters, kilns, galleries, and fans from around the world come together to connect and enjoy Bizen pottery on a deeper level.
It was established as a non-profit social DAO to support traditional crafts and regional revitalization in the Okayama-Bizen area.
Operating funds are supported by donations from purchasers of NFT-linked Bizen-yaki artworks.
Operators and supporters who build and maintain the ecosystem also participate as DAO members.
Anyone who owns an NFT-linked Bizen-yaki piece automatically becomes a BizenDAO member.
Even without an NFT, anyone can freely join and interact through our Discord community.
