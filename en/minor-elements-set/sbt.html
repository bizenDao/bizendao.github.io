SBT

SBT stands for Soulbound Token — a non-transferable, non-tradeable token, often called a "soul-bound token." At BizenDAO, we store SBTs in each artwork's TBA (Token Bound Account), creating tamper-proof records of usage history, awards, and other information that can be preserved and passed down for hundreds of years. Owners can inscribe their own experiences onto the blockchain.
