NFT

NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token — a way to tokenize irreplaceable value using blockchain technology. At BizenDAO, we capture the unique "natural glaze" patterns of each Bizen-yaki piece — as unique as fingerprints — through high-resolution photos and 3D models, embedding them in NFT metadata to serve as certificates of authenticity for irreplaceable Bizen-yaki works. We also use NFTs for traceability (provenance tracking) of each piece.
